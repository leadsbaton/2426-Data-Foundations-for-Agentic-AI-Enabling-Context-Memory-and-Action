Achieving Trusted Data: Top Trends in Data Governance

Trusted data has never been more critical as organizations accelerate AI, analytics, and self service initiatives. In fact, 62% of organizations say a lack of data governance is the main data challenge limiting AI initiatives.

Without strong data governance, AI doesn't just scale innovation—it scales risk, amplifying data quality issues, compliance exposure, bias, and untrusted outcomes across the organization. Instead of driving confidence and better decisions, AI can end up accelerating the very problems organizations are trying to solve.

  • Scaling governance with automation and AI
  • Improving data quality, lineage, and transparency
  • Turning governance into a business enabler
